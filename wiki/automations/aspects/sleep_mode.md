# Sleep Mode / Mode sommeil

## English
Sleep Mode is a “quiet house” setting that influences comfort and notifications.

### What you’ll notice
- Some sounds and notifications become less intrusive.
- Some comfort choices (heating/AC) may prioritize bedrooms at night.

Related: [Smart Heating](smart_heating.md), [Home Security & Safety](home_security_and_safety.md), [Cinema Mode](cinema_mode.md)

## Français
Le Mode sommeil est un réglage “maison calme” qui influence le confort et les notifications.

### Ce que vous remarquerez
- Certains sons et notifications deviennent moins intrusifs.
- Certains choix de confort (chauffage/clim) peuvent prioriser les chambres la nuit.

Liens : [Chauffage intelligent](smart_heating.md), [Sécurité & sûreté](home_security_and_safety.md), [Mode cinéma](cinema_mode.md)
