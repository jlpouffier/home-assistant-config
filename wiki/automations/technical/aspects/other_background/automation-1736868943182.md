### Flow (trace-style)

- Conditions (all must be true):
  - Extinction automatique des pièces is “on”
  - Modes de presence de la maison is “Occupée”
- Actions:
  - Turn off lights in Cuisine, Entrée, Salon
  - Turn off TV (Salon)
