### Summary
- Runs when: TV (Salon) to “paused”; TV (Salon) to “playing”
- Only if: TV intelligente is “on”; Mode invité is “off”; Mode Cinema (Salon) is “on”; Modes de presence de la maison is “Occupée”; Présence Rez-de-chaussée is “on”
- Then: Execute the flow below

### Flow (trace-style)
- Triggers:
  - TV (Salon) to “paused”
  - TV (Salon) to “playing”
- Conditions (all must be true):
  - TV intelligente is “on”
  - Mode invité is “off”
  - Mode Cinema (Salon) is “on”
  - Modes de presence de la maison is “Occupée”
  - Présence Rez-de-chaussée is “on”
- Actions:
  - CHOOSE (first match)
    - Branch 1
      - Check: Trigger “paused” fired
      - THEN
        - Activate Salon TV Paused (Salon), Entrée TV Paused (Entrée), Cuisine TV Paused (Cuisine)
    - Branch 2
      - Check: Trigger “playing” fired
      - THEN
        - Turn off Applique escalier (Couloir), Jardin, Cuisine, Entrée, Salon
        - Activate Salon TV Playing (Salon)
