### Summary
- Runs when: Modes de presence de la maison to “Vide”; Modes de presence de la maison from “Vide”; Home Assistant starts; Mode invité; Boost chauffage (Salle de douche)
- Only if: No extra conditions
- Then: Execute the flow below

### Flow (trace-style)
- Triggers:
  - Modes de presence de la maison to “Vide”
  - Modes de presence de la maison from “Vide”
  - Home Assistant starts
  - Mode invité
  - Boost chauffage (Salle de douche)
- Conditions (all must be true):
  - No extra conditions
- Actions:
  - CHOOSE (first match)
    - Branch 1
      - Check: Boost chauffage (Salle de douche) is “on”
      - Check: (NOT (Modes de presence de la maison is “Vide”) OR Mode invité is “on”)
      - THEN
        - Set Mode chauffage (Salle de douche) to “Boost”
    - Default
      - Set Mode chauffage (Salle de douche) to “Normal”
